*Business Rules

A Business Rule is a server-side script that runs when a record is inserted, updated, deleted, displayed, or queried. Business Rules help automate backend logic like updating fields, validating data, or creating related records.

Business Rules run on:

Before Insert

Before Update

After Insert/Update/Delete

Display

Async (background)

They are triggered even when updates come from workflows, integrations, import sets, or UI actions.
